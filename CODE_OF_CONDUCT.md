# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give helpful, specific feedback.
- Focus on ideas and code, not people.
- Be open to different viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Trolling, insults, or intentionally disruptive comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly ignoring project maintainers' guidance.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.
